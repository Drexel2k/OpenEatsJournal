PRIVACY POLICY
Last update: January 31, 2026

    INTRODUCTION
Open Eats Journal is a free, open source, data minimized and data privacy friendly eats, nutrition and calories journal app for Android. The developer does not collect any personal data via Open Eats Journal, data is not shared in any way, there are no ads and no tracking or profiling takes place. If you search for foods online to retrieve nutrition values and names, the request is sent to the Open Food Facts API  (https://world.openfoodfacts.org/). However, if the functionality is not desired or needed, you can also only search offline. Questions and suggestions regarding the privacy of Open Eats Journal can be sent to us via the e-mail address drexel.development@gmail.com.

  IV. Data storage and data deletion
a) Open Eats Journal mobile app
By default, all data (both personal and non-personal) remains on the data subject’s Android device for as long as not explicitly exported, copied and shared by the data subject. The data stored on the data subject’s device is stored in the app exclusive storage folder and can be deleted by the data subject at any time by uninstalling the app.
All data stored can be exported via the app’s export function, exports and error logs are stored on the device under Android/data/com.drexeldevelopment.openeatsjournal/files/export/ (exports) and Android/data/com.drexeldevelopment.openeatsjournal/files/log/ (error logs) and are also deleted when the app is uninstalled.
